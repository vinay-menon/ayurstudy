Diploma in Ayurveda Panchakarma Therapy

Ayurveda, the ancient Medical Science of India is a rich repository of treatment methods. Panchakarma is essentially a treatment that purifies the human body by removing the toxin accumulated within it. There are five (pancha) actions (karmas) that may be used to execute this. The body is prepared for this therapy by abhyanga and other means known as ‘Purvakarmas”.
